ABOUT US

Welcome to Melbourne Golf Club, featuring a state-of-the-art, multi-level, illuminated driving range equipped with cutting-edge Toptracer Range technology. Enhanced by a dedicated practice green and bunker area, this club also offers a cozy coffee shop and a well-stocked Golf Store.

Catering to players of all ages and skill levels, guaranteeing fun and excitement for the everyone.

TopRacer Range

Discover the thrill of golf like never before at our TopRacer Range. Floodlit range offers an immersive experience for players of all levels.

Adventure Golf

Embark on an exciting journey at our Adventure Golf, where each putt takes you through a world of prehistoric wonders and challenges.

Golf Lessons

Elevate your game with our professional golf lessons, tailored for golfers at all levels. Our experienced instructors use a personalized approach for each player
